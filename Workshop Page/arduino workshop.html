Arduino Workshop

11

In the midst of a monotonous journey of online classes ;when an
arduino board hits up the zeal to learn, the result is a whacking
participation of students. This was a compact elucidation of
Workshop conducted by the Society for Promotion of Electronics
Culture (SPEC) for the freshers. Spec conducted a workshop
on Arduino for IoT which witnessed 400+ registrations on December
24, 2020. The workshop was filled with enthusiasm of the freshmen
and the excitement to learn and grow. Spec provided a hands-on
experience with the projects and simulations and got various students interested in microcontrollers and Iot. The freshmen were made aware of every component. From basics of night light demonstration, blinking, servo and ultrasonic sensors students created a hands-on automatic hand sanitiser. The session was followed by a quiz based on the workshop and the winners are to be awarded in the SPEC Fest. It was exciting to witness the level of interaction of the students. Their queries were answered efficiently. Hence, with the proper management, support and collaboration of the team, the workshop was wrapped up in three hours, ending on an informative and refreshing note..
